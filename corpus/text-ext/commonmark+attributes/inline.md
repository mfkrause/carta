# Heading with attributes {#intro .lead}

## Sub-heading {#sub key=val}

A paragraph with `inline code`{.rust #snippet} carrying an identifier and class.

A [styled link](https://example.com){.external #home} and an
![embedded image](/cover.png){width=200 height=100} both take attributes.

Consecutive blocks merge into one attribute set: `merged`{.a}{.b #id}.

A reference [labelled link][ref]{.note} resolves and keeps its attribute.
