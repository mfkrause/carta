A [bracketed span]{.highlight #marked} wraps inline content with attributes.

Spans hold formatting: [a phrase with *emphasis* and `code`]{.note lang=en}.

A span may contain a [link to [the site](https://example.com) here]{.wrap}.

Nested spans compose: [[inner]{.a} and outer]{.b}.

An empty block is literal: [not a span]{} stays as text.

A key-value span: [config]{data-role="primary" .config}.
