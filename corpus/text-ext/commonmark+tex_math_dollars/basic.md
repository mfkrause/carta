Inline math like $a^2 + b^2 = c^2$ sits in a sentence.

Display math stands alone: $$\int_0^1 x \, dx = \frac{1}{2}$$

A bare $5 and $10 are not math, and an escaped \$x stays literal.

A closing dollar may not touch a space: $a $ and $ b$ are both literal.

Math content is verbatim: $x_1 + y_2$ keeps its underscores, and $a*b*c$ keeps its asterisks.
