A plain &copy; sign decodes in running text.

Monospace ''&amp;'' decodes, as do a <sub>&copy;</sub> subscript, a
<sup>&reg;</sup> superscript, and a <del>&copy;</del> deletion.

A link label [[start|&copy;]] keeps the entity literal, and so does a
<nowiki>&copy;</nowiki> span and a verbatim block:

<code>
&copy;
</code>
